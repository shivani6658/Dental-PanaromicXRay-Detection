About the Dental Detection Project

Panoramic Radiographs are frequently used in dentistry for treatment planning, but their interpretation manually can be both time-consuming and prone to error.
Artificial intelligence (AI) has the potential to aid in the analysis of these X-rays, thereby improving the accuracy of dental diagnoses and treatment plans.
This approach consists of a multi-step framework tailored to the task of detecting and classifying abnormal teeth.

Goal of the Project

The goal of this project is to assist dental professionals in diagnosing dental issues more efficiently and accurately.

The proposed AI-based system automates the detection and classification of dental conditions in panoramic X-rays.
It uses YOLOv5 for object detection to identify caries, restorations, and impacted teeth,
while MobileNet performs binary classification to detect caries.

The system preprocesses X-ray images through resizing, normalization, and bounding box annotation formatting
before feeding them into the models.
